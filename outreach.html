A Volte Ritornano

 Co-coordinator of this outreach program organized at CERN IdeaSquare by Markus Nordberg, Head of Resources Development and Innovation Unit at CERN. It consists of a module for ESADE Executive Management members to experience unusual environments such as CMS, ATLAS and CERN in general. The aim is to teach how collaborative behavior and leadership work in an international scientific collaboration. We eventually show also how society might benefit from fundamental physics field over the long run.

Liceo Ruffini in Gita al CERN

 This activity, contributed to the “alternanza scuola-lavoro” program. I organized conferences and seminars at CERN for the students. I guided them in the main experimental areas at CERN: AMS control room, CMS P5, Microcosm and Globe on Science and Innovation

Lab2Go

 Project of collaboration between Sapienza University and High schools in the Lazio region to facilitate the spread of experimental physics in school labs.

CMS Master Classes

 Serie of one-day long lessons to high school students. During these lessons I teach the students how to interface with the CMS software used to analyze real collision events and how to recognize and selected basic signal signatures like Z boson decays to two muons or electrons

CMS Virtual Visit

 CMS Virtual Visits offer students, teachers and the general public a unique opportunity to explore the experimental site of the CMS detector. Through a web-based videoconference, CMS scientists interact with visitors in their native language, explain the physics and technology behind the experiment, and answer their questions
